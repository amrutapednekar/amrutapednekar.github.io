At first, due to social stigma many the local people, opposed idea of the Ability center at the city. The requested to change location. The letters were send the founders for evacuating the place. There was oppose to purchase the product. It was a real challenging situation and threat, which could have stopped the execution of noble vision

Founders sought the need of awareness about the leprosy patient. It was necessity of having sensitization in the community, which will enable to have peaceful life for patients as a dignified citizen.

The team came with innovative solutions. Every Sunday, Prabhatferi A motivating morning walk started, the group mainly enchant the patriotic or motivational on the road of city. They started cleaning the temples, cleaning the roads, and filling the potholes on the roads. This created a positive impact on the community and the sensitization helped to acceptance.

At first the milk produced from cows, at Udyogdham was not accepted by community. It was sold to sweet makers directly. However days passed and community started to purchase various products like Tamarind (Chinch), bamboo, milk, trees, mango grafting.

Need of more work: As per government figure, India has 55 percent leprosy patients i.e. close to 2.3 Lacs. Many organization are working for there rehabilitation and eradicating the decease by medicine and social change. There is a need of many volunteers who can support for the change.
