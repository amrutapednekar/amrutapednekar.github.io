As narrated by Padma Chaphekar, with the instinct of developing ability center for the disabled, Dada Chaphekar had first meeting with Mr. Lagu. Mr BB Mehendale Govt officer of Public welfare department. Babasaheb kale and other member of Chapekar Family.

Aim was to create a hall in the land for ability center. The first shade was made from corrugated steel sheet, a young entrepreneur in city named Prakash Baldota donated those sheet and helped to construct the shed. The first five members named Rashid, Lakshman, Maridas, Agatrao, Mehendale made the shade with the woods, and sheets.

National chemical laboratory at pune donated the rubber moulds and powder for making the chalks. They also provided the training of making the chalks. Afterwards pune municipal corporation gave the order of 5000 flat files, TELCO (now Tata Motors) gave the order of box file. And the ability center started.

Data Chaphekar was ignited with the thought of dream ability center, where the view was society should not look at disabled people with the view of charity but to pay the returns to disabled for the product and services provided at equal quality of able person do.

It was critical task of maintaining the health of the patient The nearest medical facility was at Kondhava at north-east of pune city almost 40 KM away at that time.
