Late Anant Gopal Chaphkear, an Electrical Engineer by education tried his hands at agriculture joined New Prakash Mills at Dhule,Maharashtra and received the most unfortunate blow by the hands of destiny.

He contracted Leprosy, the most dreadful, feared disease. He was forced to quit his employment and return to his native place, Talegaon,Pune, Maharashtra.

Financial position of Anant Chaphekar was sound and thence; Mr Anant Chaphekar survived and maintained his dignity. But, he could not ignore the woe of his brethren.

His inquisitiveness to understand the disease and the consequential wrath of the society, the hardships caused on the patients fuelled his compassionate heart.

Impact of Book

At the same time, by accident, he got the opportunity to read a book by Henry Viscardi Give us the tools, a story about a company run by disabled after the world war founded to give dignity and self reliance to the disabled.

It clearly said that unless the disables run a business similar to management practice as the non-disable does, there won't be a dignity for the disabled person.

A friend of Dada Chaphekar , Mr Ravindra Desai has translated the book Marathi language as Hat Na Pasaru Kadhi.

It was The story of Ability center that sowed the seeds of Udyogdham. Mr Chaphekar's wife, Smt Padma Chaphekar and many like-minded people have supported this cause making it possible for Udyogdham to complete its journey.
