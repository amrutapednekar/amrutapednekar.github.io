Aniket Niketan

Stay For Leprosy Patients

Aniket Niketan is a hostel for the children of leprosy-affected families in Maharashtra, where the leprosy affected families are not able to raise up there pupil due to poor income and social stigma towards them. It is effort to nurture the child from unprivileged condition to self-sufficient citizen of country.

Aniket Niketan Based on our rich experience years with leprosy patients, udygodham founders realized that the children of leprosy patients were in more adverse conditions and the incident of leprosy in their families posed a threat to their future. This Aniket Niketan was established for the children of leprosy patients.

Girls and boys from 1st Standard to College education are staying at hostel and developing self for better tomorrow. Along with conventional education, special efforts are taken for there development in areas likes gymnasium (Mallakham), drawing, music, farming.
