Udyogdham leprosy center was established in 1975 at Talegaon, nearly 20km from Pune, Maharashtra by late Mr. Anant Gopal alias Dada Chaphkear, Late Nathubhau Bhegade and Smt Padmatai Chaphekar

Our vision is to provide shelter and dignified life through workshops to all-cured leprosy patients who are not readily accepted by their family members and society at large. It works primarily to give them dignity by working for their rehabilitation.
